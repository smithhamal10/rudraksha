OUR JOURNEY

Our Story

Shiva Shakti Dingla Rudraksha Bhandar was founded with a sacred mission to bring the divine energy of authentic Rudraksha beads to devotees worldwide. Our journey began over two decades ago when our founder, deeply inspired by the spiritual significance of Rudraksha, decided to dedicate his life to sourcing and sharing these sacred beads.

What started as a small collection has now grown into one of Nepal's most trusted sources for genuine Rudraksha. We work directly with traditional collectors and maintain the highest standards of authenticity, ensuring that each bead carries the divine blessings it is meant to bestow.
