Frequently Asked Questions?

Rudraksha beads are seeds from the Rudraksha tree, believed to possess spiritual and medicinal properties. They are highly revered in Hinduism, particularly by devotees of Lord Shiva, who associate them with protection, peace, and spiritual growth.

Yes, we guarantee that all our Rudraksha beads are 100% genuine and ethically sourced from sustainable farms. We ensure fair trade practices and rigorous quality checks for authenticity.

We offer a wide range of Rudraksha products, including single beads (from 1 Mukhi to 21 Mukhi), malas (prayer beads), bracelets, pendants, and custom-designed jewelry, all crafted with genuine Rudraksha.

We offer a 7-day return and exchange policy for all our products, provided they are in their original condition and packaging. Please refer to our detailed return policy page for more information.

All our Rudraksha beads come with a certificate of authenticity. We also provide information on how to identify genuine Rudraksha beads, including their natural lines (mukhi), texture, and weight, to help you verify their authenticity.

Yes, we provide delivery services across all major locations in Nepal. We also offer international shipping to various countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on your location.
